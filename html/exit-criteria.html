E.g., if the TSC designates a project as mature, does that mean it follows a consistent change control process? E.g., that any designated release has been reviewed via the defined gerritt reviewers? I.e., it's followed a change control process and release process?

jeremysevareid
2016-06-13 17:01
The reason to be explicit is that the discussions have made clear that some folks may misinterpret "mature" as an attestation. Following a release process is not just a DevOps thing. Often, people assume that a project following a release process has change controls, quality assurance and fit-for-purpose attestations being made.

jeremysevareid
2016-06-13 17:05
I think this will be particularly important for Hyperledger, given the visibility and the potential for use with financial transactions. Therefore, I'd suggest making that distinction between project's maturity and what's being attested to and by whom front-and-center.
