The Interledger project doesn’t use Slack (the community decided against it) but I am happy to answer any questions and keep the dialogue between our projects open here if that’s useful to you? We use Gitter: https://gitter.im/interledger/Lobby and IRC: http://irc.w3.org #interledger We also have two mailing lists at W3C () and IETF (). Joining the W3C community group will cover you as a contributor and sign you up to the mailing list: https://www.w3.org/community/interledger/ The IETF list is a bit more old skool: https://www.ietf.org/mailman/listinfo/ledger
