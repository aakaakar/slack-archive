@urgsurez The version of blockchain-explorer that works with Fabric v0.6 is at https://github.com/hyperledger/blockchain-explorer . Do a *git clone https://github.com/hyperledger/blockchain-explorer * and then follow the directions in *blockchain-explorer/explorer_1/README.md*. I followed the instructions in the Installation section and only had to make one deviation from them- I was installing as root, and *bower* did not like being installed as root until I performed this command: ```echo '{ "allow_root": true }' > /root/.bowerrc``` as specified here: http://serverfault.com/questions/548537/cant-get-bower-working-bower-esudo-cannot-be-run-with-sudo *Note:* I just ran the explorer natively on my OS and not in a Docker container so I didn't do the last step of building the hyperledger/blockchain-explorer Docker image, so I don't know if there are any pitfalls in that step.
